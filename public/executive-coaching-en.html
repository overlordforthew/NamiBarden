About Nami

About Your Coach

I was born in Nagasaki, Japan — and by 17, I was training at the Princess Grace Ballet Academy in Monaco, then in Paris to become a professional ballet dancer. At 20, I made a sharp turn: moved to the U.S., studied business at Fort Lewis College in Colorado, and graduated as a Dean's List Honor Student. After working at a foreign-affiliated company in Tokyo and spending over 10 years in her husband's real estate investment firm in Hawaii — handling 50+ investment, sales, and development projects — she also earned a Hawaii real estate license. She traveled to India five times to study consciousness-based approaches to inner well-being. In 2018, she went independent as a seminar instructor and personal counselor.

Since then, she has guided CEOs, physicians, attorneys, investors, and athletes around the world in aligning their inner state to unlock their true potential. Her book, The Heart's Lesson That Billionaires Around the World Arrive At, became a bestseller in Japan. Through seminars in Hawaii, Tokyo, Fukuoka, and Nagano, three executive retreats, and over 1,000 individual counseling sessions, she has helped transform the inner lives of countless professionals.
