About Nami

About Your Coach

I was born in Nagasaki, Japan — and by 17, I was training at the Princess Grace Ballet Academy in Monaco, then in Paris, pursuing my dream of becoming a professional ballet dancer. At 20, I made a sharp turn: moved to the U.S., studied business at Fort Lewis College in Colorado, and graduated as a Dean's List Honor Student. From there, I worked at a global firm in Tokyo, then spent over a decade in Hawaii running real estate alongside my husband — leading 50+ investment and development projects and earning my Hawaii real estate license. Five trips to India deepened my study of consciousness-based inner work at its source. In 2018, I went independent.

Since then, I've worked with CEOs, doctors, lawyers, investors, and athletes — helping them get out of their own way and into their full potential. My first book became a bestseller in Japan. I've led seminars across Hawaii, Tokyo, Fukuoka, and Nagano, hosted three executive retreats, and done over 1,000 one-on-one sessions.
