Story & Credentials

A Life in Motion.
1,000+ Lives Transformed.

I was born in Nagasaki, Japan — and by 17, I was training at the Princess Grace Ballet Academy in Monaco, then in Paris, pursuing my dream of becoming a professional ballet dancer. At 20, I made a sharp turn: moved to the U.S., studied business at Fort Lewis College in Colorado, and graduated as a Dean's List Honor Student. From there, I worked in Tokyo's corporate world before spending over a decade in Hawaii running real estate — leading 50+ investment and development projects, handling everything from design to logistics, and earning my Hawaii real estate license along the way. In 2018, I stepped out on my own. Five trips to India deepened my study of consciousness-based practices at their source.

Since going independent, I've worked with over 12,000 people — executives, doctors, lawyers, investors, athletes — through seminars, online courses, executive retreats, and more than 1,000 one-on-one sessions. My first book became a bestseller in Japan. I've led seminars across Hawaii, Tokyo, Fukuoka, and Nagano, and hosted three executive retreats.

And I'm still at it. In 2025, my family and I sailed from Greece across the Mediterranean and the Atlantic on a 58-foot catamaran. Still out here — still teaching, still learning.
