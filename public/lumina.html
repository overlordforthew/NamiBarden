Questions

Before you subscribe

When will I be charged?

Your card is collected at checkout, but the paid renewal starts only after the 7-day trial unless you cancel first.

Where do I cancel?

Inside Lumina, open Profile and choose Manage billing. That takes you to the Stripe billing portal.

What email should I use?

Use the same email for Lumina and billing. That keeps activation, billing management, and support much cleaner.

What stays private?

Your Lumina reflections stay in the Lumina app. Subscription and payment records live in NamiBarden and Stripe for billing and accounting.
